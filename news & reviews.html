Gunung Mulu National Park

Gunung Mulu National Park offers hikers and trail an escape to paradise. The park's lush green trails wind through dense vegetation, leading adventurers to breathtaking viewpoints that overlook the majestic limestone karsts and the verdant valley below. From exploring intricate cave systems, like the awe-inspiring Sarawak Chamber, to conquering the challenging ascent of Mount Mulu, the park presents an unparalleled opportunity to immerse oneself in the untouched beauty of the wilderness, making it a must-visit destination for those seeking an unforgettable hiking and trailing experience.

Penang Hill

Penang Hill: A hiker's haven on Penang Island, offering scenic trails and stunning vistas for an unforgettable nature escape. Ascend to the summit for panoramic views of the coastline and city below.
